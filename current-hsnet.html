Subscribe to H&S Net

To "subscribe" or "unsubscribe" from any of the lists, please follow the instructions found on the page links below:

  • HonorsNet: https://lists.service.ohio-state.edu/mailman/listinfo/honorsnet
  • ScholarsNet: https://lists.service.ohio-state.edu/mailman/listinfo/scholarsnet
  • HSFacNet: https://lists.service.ohio-state.edu/mailman/listinfo/hsfacnet

We do our best to keep you informed about what’s going on at the Center and on campus through Honors & Scholars Net. Look for an email from us every Monday with tons of information about activities and special opportunities that are available to you as Honors & Scholars students.
