Volunteer

Over 15,000 students have graduated from The Ohio State University Honors & Scholars Programs.   The Honors & Scholars Center is committed to staying in touch with our graduates and wants to celebrate your success. Stay connected to your alma mater, particularly to Honors & Scholars, by becoming an alumni connector, facilitating an alumni fireside chat or joining the newly forming Honors & Scholars Alumni Society.  We look forward to hearing from you!

Alumni Connections

Are you willing to serve as a career resource for current Honors & Scholars students? If so then we have just the program for you – the Alumni Connections Program. Alumni agree to have a photo and basic contact information listed on our website so that students can contact you with career-related questions. This is a wonderful opportunity for you to share your experiences with current students and serve as a resource. If you are interested in serving please contact Cheria V. Dial at dial.3@osu.edu or 614.292.3135.

Alumni Fireside Chats

Facilitating an alumni fireside chat is a great way for you to come home to Kuhn and share your experiences with current students. Topics are often career related and focus on things about which you are passionate that might also appeal to Honors & Scholars students. If you are interested in conducting an alumni fireside chat, please contact Cheria V. Dial via email at dial.3@osu.edu or give her a call at the University Honors & Scholars Center – 614.292.3135.

Sample Alumni Fireside Chats

  • The Case of the Gospel of Judas with Roger A. Weber
  • What Can I Do with a Law Degree? With Joe Miller and Scott Powell
  • Building a Career in International Volunteerism with Ann M. Merrill
  • Running for Office: How to Mount a Local Campaign with John Patrick Carney

Alumni Society

We have plans to support the formation of an Honors & Scholars alumni society. If you are interested in being involved with this initiative, please email Cheria V. Dial at dial.3@osu.edu.
